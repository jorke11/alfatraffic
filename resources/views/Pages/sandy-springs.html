DIRECTIONS

We are in Sandy Springs off Roswell Road. ALFA is located in the same shopping center as the Georgia DDS license office, Sandy Springs Diner and Tony's Sports Bar.

 

5-minute drive from Roswell

5-minute drive from Dunwoody

10-minute drive from East Cobb

15-minute drive from Buckhead

20-minute drive from Downtown Atlanta

 

From GA 400, Exit #6.  Travel 1/4 mile West on Northridge Road to Roswell Road.  Turn right onto Roswell Road and ALFA is 1/2 mile North on left side of Roswell Road.

 

From I-285, Exit #25, Travel 6 miles North on Roswell Road towards Sandy Springs.  ALFA is on the left side of Roswell Road.
